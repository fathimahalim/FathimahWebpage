🔗 Each slot is conducted by each group that has been divided in my class.

🔗 I hold a job as a Technical Bureau. So, I gained experience in handling every technique that runs throughout this event.

🔗 Our webinar received a large audience of all ages and each slot received a lively and fun response from the audience

💻 For my group, we conduct for the second slot which is The E-sport Talk & Quiz slot.

💻 We gave a talk on how E-sports can be turned into a career.

💻 In addition to giving talks, we also provide quiz competitions by offering cash prizes to the first 5 winners.

💻 However, it such a great experience and this webinar gives me more self -confidence especially in communication skills that is when I need to talk to a lot of people.
